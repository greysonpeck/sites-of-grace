            Equipment & Magic
              +

            Talismans in Elden Ring are accessories that bestow a variety of special effects. They're dropped by Enemies or Bosses, looted from chests, pilfered from corpses, or purchased from Merchants.

            Talismans cannot be upgraded; however, more powerful versions of some talismans are obtainable. They're often recognizable by their bolster text (e.g. Talisman Name +1), with exceptions such as the Great-Jar's Arsenal (a direct upgrade to the Arsenal Charm).

            Talismans are allowed in Player Trade.

            Shadow of the Erdtree New Talismans

            With the release of Shadow of the Erdtree, 39 new Talismans are added to the already stacked 115 Talismans in the base game. This leads to a total of 154 Talismans available between the base game and the new content added with the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC.

            Of the new Talismans in Shadow of the Erdtree, some are brand new, providing new and exciting unique effects, while others are empowered versions of previous classics already present the base version of Elden Ring.

            This page is up to date with all this new content. Players will be able to search and find all Shadow of the Erdtree New Talismans here, as well as to learn about their Effects, Best uses & combinations, Builds that utilize them and much more regarding the new Talismans added with the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC!

            Increasing Talisman Slots in Elden Ring

            Obtaining Talisman Pouches increases the number of talisman equip slots. Three can be obtained for a total of four equip slots. These are obtained after defeating a select few Bosses:

            Talisman Pouches are rewarded, in no particular order: 

            1. Upon defeating Margit, the Fell Omen in Stormhill, [Map link] 
              or Morgott, the Omen King in Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Map link]
            2. After speaking to Finger Reader Enia, after acquiring two Great Runes,
              or found in the Twin Maiden Husks' shop, if missed from Enia.
            3. Upon defeating Godfrey, First Elden Lord (Golden Shade) in Leyndell, Royal Capital. [Map link]

            All Legendary Talismans in Elden Ring

Unless you solely use Bloodhound’s Step or something this is a no-brainer)
                                                                                                                                            -Erdtree's Favor +2 (A little more health, quite a bit more stamina and carry weight all-in-one. What's there to gripe about?)
                                                                                                                                            -Lord of Blood's Exultation (Bleed is absurdly accessible, easy to proc and this boosts ALL of your damage. Another no-brainer unless you specifically don’t use bleed or if fighting Radagon/Elden Beast)
                                                                                                                                            -Godfrey Icon (Most accessible boost to spell damage if they’re chargeable, which a lot of the best spells are. Combined w/ Shard of Alexander chargeable skills can turn into instant win buttons.)
                                                                                                                                            -Green Turtle Talisman (Stamina: you need it to move, you need it to fight, you need it to fu- er I mean it *really* helps you catch your breath. For moving, and fighting, yeah..)
                                                                                                                                            -Claw Talisman (Jumping attacks are very powerful as it is, this turns them into damage BOMBS)

                                                                                                                                            A Tier - Very excellent talismans a few of which are borderline S tier

                                                                                                                                            -Crimson Amber Medallion +2 (Health: it's what keeps you alive, now you got a ****-ton more of it. Praise the Sun)
                                                                                                                                            -Kindred of Rot’s Exultation (All the benefits of LOB’s with the challenge of Poison/Rot being trickier to proc repeatedly.)
                                                                                                                                            -Millicent’s Prosthesis (Basically two talismans for the price of one, it’s great. Recommend running also the Rotten Wingsword Insignia and Thorny Cracked Tear to maximize returns)
                                                                                                                                            -Radagon Icon (When a single frame dictates whether a spell lands this is a game changer. Would be S tier if max casting speed didn’t require you to level Dex or equip a specific staff)
                                                                                                                                            -Flock’s Canvas Talisman (More free damage is always great, though an 8% boost isn’t exactly huge unless your incant scaling is already very high.)
                                                                                                                                            -Graven-Mass Talisman (Same as above but with sorcery, duh.)
                                                                                                                                            -Old Lord’s Talisman (Amazing talisman. Loses out on S tier only due to it providing diminished value to shorter buffs like Flame Grant Me Strength, Howl of Shabriri, Black Flame Blade and certain AoW’s)
                                                                                                                                            -Dagger Talisman (If you’re into parrying or backstabbing this will give you a LOT of one-shot kills especially in PvP)


                                                                                                                                            B Tier - Talismans that are great but becoming more niche and play-style dependent

                                                                                                                                            -Taker’s Cameo (Very useful to help preserve flasks particularly in clearing dungeons, useless in duels and arena Boss fights of course)
                                                                                                                                            -Godskin Swaddling Cloth (Lot of potential synergy and shines on fast weapons. Mixed bag on slower weapons or if you’re one-shotting mobs - which is a good problem to have)
                                                                                                                                            -Rotten Wingsword Insignia (Max buff is great but takes work getting there and falls off quickly just like the Prosthesis. Stack all of its synergies to make it more worthwhile)
                                                                                                                                            -Bull-Goat Talisman (More poise is great but with the recent PvP update unless you already have really high poise I would recommend wearing heavier armor first)
                                                                                                                                            -Roar Medallion (Great for the certain spells and skills it applies to. Turns Greyoll’s Roar into a nuke)
                                                                                                                                            Both Seed Talismans (Stronger flasks are wonderful all-around, but only if you really need them.)
                                                                                                                                            -Cerulean Amber Medallion +2 (Now you have even more FP for your Comet Azur, though you’ll likely want high Mind to get meaningful value out of it)
                                                                                                                                            -Viridian Amber Medallion +2 (Yay, more stamina for panic-rolling through Malenia’s Waterfowl Dance)
                                                                                                                                            -Both Ritual Talismans (Gotta stay at full health for them to pull their weight sure, but that’s what your 14 flasks and regen spells are for!)
                                                                                                                                            -Spear Talisman (Shield-pokers rejoice! Very good in PvP if you’re running any thrusting weapon)
                                                                                                                                            -Both Seed Talismans (Your flasks are stronger, pretty darn efffective. Crimson is obviously worthless in Duels)

                                                                                                                                            C Tier - Talismans that are good in their niche but likely won’t provide ample value otherwise

                                                                                                                                            -All Scarseals/Soreseals (Great for hitting requirements and gouging stats to pump up vigor early game, negation penalty becomes less and less justified as you level up and should never be slotted ultra long-term)
                                                                                                                                            -All Scorpion Charms (Ok for boosting spells, terrible for weapons w/ split damage as damage increase is too negligible to justify the steep negation penalty. D tier in PvP)
                                                                                                                                            -All Drake Talismans (Better off boosting your hp pool and using relevant elemental negation spells, unless you’re *really* wanting to max out for a hard boss or if you just don’t use spells)
                                                                                                                                            -All Heirloom Talismans (Fine early game to help you meet requirements, but become less and less relevant the more you hone your build and should be completely obsolete by late game)
                                                                                                                                            -Gold Scarab (Want to make rune farming an even more pain-free experience? Go nuts.)
                                                                                                                                            -Silver Scarab (Item discovery is such an irritating mechanic, this does help but not much by itself. Hope you have good Arcane and some fowl feet because you’ll need them too)
                                                                                                                                            -Ancestral Spirit Horn (Can help keep your FP topped off if you use a lot of spells/skill, but you’re almost always better off just slotting the Cerulean Seed or Amber Medallion Talismans)
                                                                                                                                            -Carian Filigreed Crest (Good if your Mind is really low for skills. Otherwise might be better off running a Cerulean Amber Medallion or Seed Talisman, or even the above before considering this.)
                                                                                                                                            -Companion Jar (Boosts your throwing pots sure but those likely aren’t and should not be your primary source of DPS)
                                                                                                                                            -Moon of Nokstella (Might catch flak for it not being higher but when you have a staggering 10 spell slots already two more will likely not provide ample value for most people)
                                                                                                                                            -Both Feathered Branch Swords (These can REALLY save you in a pinch, but to take up a slot and provide zero value until you’re almost dead and have to stay that way to keep it active never sat right with me.)
                                                                                                                                            -Axe Talisman (Charged R2’s are even more ridiculously strong than before, provided you don’t whiff or get staggered out of them. Extreme luck needed to land one in PvP)
                                                                                                                                            -Both Arrow Talismans (Extra arrow range and damage is good and all, though find it tough to justify dedicating 1-2 slots for just a bow and only a handful of spells/skills. Good but niche)
                                                                                                                                            -Curved Sword Talisman (Great for PvE, not ranked higher since guard counters are next to useless in PvP outside of luck)
                                                                                                                                            -Lance Talisman (Great for boss fights where you can ride Torrent, for mobs it’s more straight-forward to hop off and fight them so I’d say it’s situational)
                                                                                                                                            -Graven School/Faithful Canvas Talismans (Ok, but boost is so small it is hardly noticeable unless your spell scaling is VERY high. Only slot if you're stacking with their stronger counter-part)

                                                                                                                                            D Tier - Extremely niche talismans, you likely have a very specific reason if you're using any of them. Some are borderline F Tier

                                                                                                                                            -All Horn Charms (Extremely few scenarios where any status effect is a huge enough problem to waste a slot on. Lake of Rot is one of very few exceptions)
                                                                                                                                            -Prince of Death Cyst (See Above)
                                                                                                                                            -Crepus’s Vial (PvE utility debatable. Enemy AI is oblivious enough during sneak already, no meaningful utility in PvP)
                                                                                                                                            -Concealing Veil (Utility also debatable for similar reasons above. Might be better for PvP but I’ll never slot it)
                                                                                                                                            -Both Trick Mirrors (PvP invasion specific and they’re basically just for trolling invaders. Waste of space in my opinion but not entirely useless I guess.)
                                                                                                                                            -Both Assassin Dagger Talismans (Extra HP/FP on crits is nice and all but it’s a big hassle to make these pull their weight when you can just run an Amber Medallion or Seed Talisman)
                                                                                                                                            -Perfumer’s Talisman (Only works on offensive aromatics and you can only hold 10 total. VERY niche)
                                                                                                                                            -Blue Dancer Charm (Max benefit is good but requires you to have an extremely low equip load which is likely not worth it outside of very niche play-styles)
                                                                                                                                            -Longtail Cat Talisman (Only protects against non-lethal falls, in a game where the majority of falls are either lethal or don’t damage you much/at all to begin with. So it’s a no from me, DOG.)
                                                                                                                                            -Primal Glintstone Blade (Last FP related talisman you’ll want to consider. Almost never worth the hp handicap when you can run a number of better talismans that have no drawbacks)
                                                                                                                                            -Blessed Dew Talisman (Go ahead and slot this early game if you want, but once you clear Limgrave throw it in the trash and never look back.)
                                                                                                                                            -Twinblade Talisman (Only buffs your final attack which requires more time and stamina than you might want to spend for a tiny return. Ironically more useful on colossal weapons than twinblades.)
                                                                                                                                            -Hammer Talisman (Useless in PvP since almost no one blocks, only really good against mobs w/ shields in PvE which make up a minority. Even then just bait an attack, roll behind and backstab them)
                                                                                                                                            -Greatshield Talisman (Fine for shield-pokers, but don’t bother using this on anything smaller than a greatshield. It won’t make a meaningful difference. Likely better off just rolling anyways. Very niche)
                                                                                                                                            -Crucible Scale Talisman (Reduces incoming critical damage, fine. How about just don’t put yourself in situations to get criticaled? If you’re getting tagged enough to warrant using this you have a skill issue.)
                                                                                                                                            -Crucible Feather Talisman (Highly mixed bag, increases roll i-frames but punishes you heavily if you mess up your timing with a very steep negation penalty. I’d personally just avoid it altogether)
                                                                                                                                            -Shabriri's Woe (Drawing aggro has its place, but those places are likely VERY few and far between outside of specific strats)


                                                                                                                                            F Tier - Bottom of the barrel. Why would you even look in the general direction of these things?..

                                                                                                                                            -Crucible Knot Talisman (Out of hundreds of hours of gameplay I have been headshot maybe twice and only during invasions. Waste of a slot 99.9999999% of the time so don’t even bother)
                                                                                                                                            -Daedicar’s Woe (Seriously? What rhyme or reason is there for you to use this other than to self-handicap for god knows why… masochist.)
                                                                                                                                            -Sacrifical Twig (Runes are literally EVERYWHERE. Kill bosses. Find Golden Runes. Farm Greyoll. Farm the Albinaurics at Mohg’s. A quality of life thing sure but 100% unnecessary. I’ve never used one)
                                                                                                                                            Entwining Umbilical Cord (Not even obtainable but all it would’ve done is slightly alter the way your character model behaves so I say whatever)

                                                                                                                                            This is merely my take and some will definitely disagree so please feel free to discuss below in the comments.

                                                                                                                                            Cheers!

                                                                                                                                                                                                                      I just hate that after 4 souls games that nearly all the talismans are exact carbon copies of rings we've had since Demons Souls. The only thing that's changed is the names. I feel like we could've gotten so many cool unique effects that would be awesome in a souls open world game. Like sure include all these talismans, these effects have been in the souls games forever. But they should of branched out a lot more.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                      A few ideas for talismans I had.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Gather twice as materials when harvesting items. (Great for when you're exploring.)
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - Increase torrents health by x%
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - increase damage by x% when you have heavy equip load
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - increase damage by x% during day (have a nighttime version too) maybe have an extreme negative effect with it.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - increase strength of spirit summons but they cost twice as much.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - gain a notification when near a cave or dungeon
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - fp Regen
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - killing enemies grants a momentary boost to stamina recovery these effect stacks
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - x% chance to recover arrows from slain foes
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - wearing full set of armor grants x effect
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - weapons deal increased poise damage but you take increased poise damage
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - increase crouch speed by x% critical hits are silent and faster
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - decrease number of enemies it takes to Regen flasks
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - gain a notification when near a site of grace
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - increase torrent move speed by x%
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - increase damage by x% when below x% stamina
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - gain a flask charge when someone rates your message
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - gain a damage boost when someone rates your message
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - spirit summons gain health when killing an enemy
                                                                                                                                                                                                                      - the list goes on and on. They could've made a bunch of exploration focused talismans, more talismans which unique effects instead of the same things we've had for years. Obviously they would have to balanced and such but the potential was there.
